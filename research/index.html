Expeditions

  • IODP Expedition 399: Building Blocks of Life, Atlantis Massif. This seafloor drilling expedition explored the basement rock underlying the Lost City hydrothermal field. A new 1.26 km borehole was drilled into the Atlantis Massif, just 800 m from the Lost City chimneys. Recovery of rock cores during drilling was very high, resulting in hundreds of samples collected for geological, chemical, and microbiological research.
  • Return to Lost City 2018. Departed Woods Hole, Massachusetts on September 8 aboard the R/V Atlantis with a team of scientists and the ROV Jason team. Returned to San Juan, Puerto Rico on October 1.
