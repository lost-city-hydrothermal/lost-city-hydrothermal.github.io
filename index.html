Lost City hydrothermal field

The Lost City is a beautiful seafloor formation with unique scientific and cultural value. It is studied by scientists around the world as a dramatic example of an ecosystem that is supported by geological and chemical processes inside the Earth. This research has implications for the origin of life and the search for life in the solar system.
